Typography and text patterns

Text used on the Chez Nous site consists of open source fonts linked from Google. The typefaces used for headers h1 through h3 is Josefin Sans in 100 weight. H4 headers are Roboto Slab 700. Josefin Sans is also used minimally in 400 weight elsewhere. The paragraph font used is Roboto Slab in 400 weight.
